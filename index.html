9 Simple Tips to Build a Strong Reading Habit

📖

Read every day

Even 10-15 minutes can help grow your vocabulary, focus, and imaginatics.

🧠

Choose books that challenge your thinking

Reading outside your comfort zone expends your mind.

🌎

Explore different cultures through stories

Fiction and non-fiction from around the world dopen your understanding of humanity.

📝

Take notes or highlight key insights

Jotting down ideas helps you reflect and remember better

📅

Set reading goals

Whether it's one book a month or a chapter a day. Consistency builds a reading habit.

🔕

Minimize distractions while reading

Find a quiet space where your mind can fully engage with the book.

📚

Revisit your favourite books

Some books speak differently to you as different times in life.

🤝

Join a book club or reading group

Discussing books with others eariches your perspective and motivation.

📃

Organize your reakding list

Keep track of what you've read and what you want to read. It helps you stay motivated and plan you next book easily.
